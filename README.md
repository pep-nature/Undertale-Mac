## 🧠 What is Undertale for Mac?

Undertale is a 2D role-playing game where players control a human who falls into an underground world filled with monsters. Unlike traditional RPGs, players can choose to fight, spare, or befriend enemies, and every decision affects the story.

It is designed to:

* Deliver a unique RPG experience
* Let players choose violence or mercy
* Feature emotional storytelling
* Offer multiple endings
* Include memorable characters
* Encourage replayability
